Info

Scootchbots are a species of autonomous agents invented by supergenius programmer Michael Trestman in 2014. Each consists of a segmented body with antennae capable of detecting fluctuations in the ambient quantum-gravitic field. Indestructable beings of pure data, they wander the Milky Way Galaxy, riding the ebb and flow of cosmic energy, ever in search of a balance between yin and yang, love and hate, twi and laa.
Nostradamus prophesied that scootchbots would eventually fill the cosmos, bringing about the end of all things, but Nicodemus foretold that only scootchbots could save the galaxy by defeating the spacewolf that dwells in the supermassive black hole at the center of the Milky Way, devouring star systems and drinking the soul of night .

scootch bots simulator controls


p to pause and o to unpause
the green circle (yin) attracts the bots
w, s, a, d to move; c to stop.

the red circle (yang) repels the bots
up, down, left, right to move; space to stop.

g to turn gravity on and t to turn it off
f to turn friction on and r to turn it off
y to turn tracing on and u to turn it off
